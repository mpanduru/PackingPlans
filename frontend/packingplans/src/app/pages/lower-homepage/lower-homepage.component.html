Step 1: Choose your Destination

Our website offers a list of the most popular travel destinations from around the world. You can browse through these locations and get inspired by the possibilities for your next trip. To narrow down the list of locations and find the perfect match for your interests, you can select specific tags that represent the types of experiences you desire. For example, if you're a beach lover, you can choose the "beach" tag to see destinations renowned for their stunning coastlines.

Step 2: Select the Time

If you know where, then all you need to worry for now is when. After you picked the location from our wide selection of destinations, you need to choose the time interval when you want to travel next. You can make your vacation as long as you want, but make sure to specify both start date and end date of it.

Step 3: Customize your trip

Once the location and time frame are set, you can embark on a truly personalized journey by adding custom activities. Our website empowers you to create a one-of-a-kind itinerary by incorporating activities that reflect your interests, preferences, and desired experiences. Make sure you have everything planed: add the time, name and maybe a short description for all your activities.

Step 4: Check your calendar

Once you are satisfied with your trip plan, you can save it on the website. Saving the trip stores all the chosen locations, time frame, and activities in your user profile. The website provides a visual representation of your trip itinerary, with each day displaying the scheduled activities. You can easily navigate through the calendar, view specific days, and make any necessary edits or adjustments.
